# V2-F Playoffs Handoff

Status: Shipped 2026-04-28.

Implemented a top-4 single-elimination playoff layer for Manager Mode. Newly created manager seasons are explicitly marked `top4_single_elimination`; old and in-flight saves without that marker keep regular-season-only behavior.

Playoff semifinal and final matches are stored in the normal `scheduled_matches` queue. Brackets and final season outcomes persist in additive v10 tables. The manager flow materializes semifinals after regular-season completion, materializes the final after semifinals, and persists the playoff-final champion before the off-season ceremony.

The champion beat and player championship rollups now prefer `season_outcomes`, with standings-leader fallback for legacy seasons.
